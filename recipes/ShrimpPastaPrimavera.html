Creamy Shrimp Pasta Primavera

This recipe covers the necessary steps and ingridents used to make Shrimp Pasta Primavera. It is really flexible and you can swap out ingredients and change measurements to your liking. The prep time will be approximately 20 minutes and the cooking time is also around 20 minutes. However, this may be longer depending on the ingredients that were replaced or other external factors like cooking equipment. In total this process should make 40 minutes total.

In total you will expect to receive 4 servings for regular adults. Per Serving Macronutrients are:

  • 495 calories
  • 32g of fat
  • 26g of carbs
  • 24g of protein

Ingredients

  • 1/2 (8 ounces) package of spaghetti
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 splash white wine (Optional)
  • 1 pinch dried oregano (Optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (Optional)
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil, or to taste (Optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.

  2. Step 2

    3. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onions and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Stir in mushrooms and bell pepper; cook until bell pepper has softened and mushrooms are lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Add shrimp, white wine, and oregano. Cook until shrimp are almost cooked through and turning pink, 3 to 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add cream and butter. Simmer, stirring constantly, for 8 minutes. Add cooked spaghetti, mix to combine, and turn off heat. If cream sauce is too thin, thicken with flour. Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and basil.
