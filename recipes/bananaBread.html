Banana Bread

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor. The prep time will be approximately 15 minutes and the cooking time is also around 1 minutes. However, this may be longer depending on the ingredients that were replaced or other external factors like cooking equipment. In total this process should make 1 hr and 15 minutes total.

In total you will expect to receive 12 servings for regular adults. Per Serving Macronutrients are:

  • 299 calories
  • 9g of fat
  • 35g of carbs
  • 4g of protein

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 3/4 of cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups of mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
